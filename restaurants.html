Restaurants

FINE DINING

KINGS STEAKBAR

Extraordinary, melt-on-the-mouth steak, with great complexity of flavour. No one does it better than the Kings SteakBar. The city's most popular option for fine dining in Colombo. The wines are spectacular too where great beef and great reds go hand in hand. Peruse the tray of raw cuts or just for that ribeye served with a choice of sauces.


COLOMBO BUFFETS

HARBOUR COURT

The Harbour Court is The Kingsbury's all-day-dining restaurant serving an impressive array of international and fusion dishes. One of the most popular buffets in Colombo, the Harbour Court boasts a variety of live cooking stations and gourmet theatrics featuring award wining chefs from across the world.



CHINESE CUISINE

YUE CHUAN

Whether you're looking for fiery Sichuan, dim sum or dumplings, Yue Chuan is one of the finest Chinese restaurants in Colombo. We invite you to experience an extensive menu serving the best Chinese food in Colombo, coupled with glamour and excellent service. Led by Chefs who have mastered the art of Sichuan cuisine, The Beach View, Colombo hosts one of the most popular restaurants for both business lunches and family dinners.
